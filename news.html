The REECO-SOIL Project Has Officially Started!

04-06-2025

This morning we had our kick-start meeting with the REECO-SOIL team bringing together an incredible group of collaborators: Dr. Juliana Calabria-Holley (Co-PI), Prof. Francisco Javier Castilla Pascual (Co-PI), and Anushka Gupta (Research Assistant). It's fantastic to see the energy, ideas, and expertise everyone is bringing to the table!

The project, Regenerative Retrofitting Via Ecologically Active Soil Structures, explores how biologically active soils and drone-based technologies can transform existing buildings into greener, healthier, and more sustainable spaces.

Let the regenerative green retrofitting revolution begin!
